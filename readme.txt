Improve your website's search results ranking and click-through-rates (CTR) by integrating JSON-LD schema code. Works with all search engines.

SEO is an integral part of every website. Improving visibility on search engines can do wonders to the growth of your business.

> This plugin aim to make SEO creation process easy. Included are shortcodes, divi modules & gutenberg blocks that make use of the schema data. No programming experience needed.

== Installation ==

How to install the `WordPress Schema Plugin For Divi, Gutenberg & Shortcodes` plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `wp-structured-data-schema` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Article schema & how is it useful? =

[Article Schema](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/article) adds structured data to your news, blog and sport article page. 

**Article schema** can increase the changes of your page appearing in SERPs with rich snippets. 


= What is Breadcrumb schema & how is it useful? = 

[BreadcrumbList](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/breadcrumb) schema markup generates **breadcrumb rich snippets** on your search engine result pages (**SERPs**)

They are good for SEO as they make navigation easier. A user visits lot more pages before exiting. It results in lower bounce rates. 

= What is Local Business schema & how is it useful? =

[Local Business Schema](https://wptools.app/how-to/how-to-add-local-business-schema-to-wordpress/) provides your business information to search engines in structured format.

With this, your business stands to gain being featured in local search queries like "find the nearest gas station"

= What is FAQ schema & how is it useful? =

[FAQ Schema](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/faqpage) page contains a list of frequently asked questions and answers.

Implementing **FAQ Schema** more often than not leads to search engines showing rich snippets on SERP. 

= What is HowTo Schema & how is it useful? =

A [How To](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/how-to) Schema is a set of **step-by-step instructions** for your users. 

HowTo schema pages are eligible for rich snippets on SERPs

= What is Organization Schema & how is it useful? =

[Organization Schema](https://schema.org/Organization) adds schema data information about your business. This

**Organization schema** can increase the changes of your page appearing in SERPs with rich snippets and knowledge graph. 

= What is Covid-19 Special Announcement Schema & how is it useful? =

Covid-19 pandemic has disrupted schedules and other aspects of everyday life. 

It affects day-to-day activity of businesses and organizations. Businesses need to announce about any disruptions.

Such announcements could in the form of 

* Temporary closure notice
* Travel restrictions
* Revised hours
* General guidelines etc

> Search engines provides **[SpecialAnnouncement](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/data-types/special-announcements)** schema data. It shows the announcement as a rich snippet on SERP.
